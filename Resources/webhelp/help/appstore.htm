The AppStore


This feature was introduced in 1.4 Beta 4 Forkshaft 2 as a stub and was improved in 1.4 Beta 4 Forkshaft 3

The Appstore is a feature that is meant as a program/plug-in extension for the browser.

Opening the AppStore 

You can open the AppStore through the Ribbon Backstage in the Ribbon or through the Main Menu in the Menu.

To open the AppStore in the Ribbon , open the Ribbon Backstage , open the Home tab , and then click AppStore.

To open the AppStore in the Menu , navigate to the Main menu , and click Pre-release AppStore.
